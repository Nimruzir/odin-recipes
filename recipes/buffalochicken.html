Buffalo Chicken

Description

A Buffalo wing in American cuisine is an unbreaded chicken wing section (however, we will be using breast) that is generally deep-fried, then coated or dipped in a sauce consisting of a vinegar-based cayenne pepper hot sauce and melted butter prior to serving.

Ingredients

  • 4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
  • 1 (17.5 fluid ounce) bottle Buffalo wing sauce, divided
  • Â½ (1 ounce) package dry ranch salad dressing mix
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 6 hoagie rolls, split lengthwise

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Place chicken breasts into the slow cooker; pour in 3/4 of the wing sauce and ranch dressing mix.
  3. Cover and cook on Low for 6 to 7 hours.
  4. Shred chicken in the cooker with two forks. Stir in butter.
  5. Pile shredded chicken and sauce onto hoagie rolls. Serve with remaining Buffalo sauce.
  6. Serve and enjoy!
